Frequently Asked Questions

What is a virtual assistant?

A virtual assistant provides administrative support to clients remotely from their own office.

How do I become a virtual assistant?

Start by identifying your skills, creating a resume, and applying for VA jobs on various platforms.

What tools do I need as a virtual assistant?

You need a reliable computer, internet connection, communication tools (e.g., email, chat apps), and productivity software (e.g., task managers, document editors).
